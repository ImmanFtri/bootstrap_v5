Internship Projects

Unity Game : Guess Characters

Incorporating the Unity game engine, I adeptly modified the 2D game titled "Guess Character," which draws inspiration from the renowned anime series "One Piece." The game revolves around players spelling out the names of various characters from the anime. Skillfully developed and polished, this captivating game has been successfully published under the company's Google Play account, aiming to attract fans of all ages and anime enthusiasts alike.

Flutter Game : Flappy Head World Cup

Incorporating a captivating football theme, I creatively modified the Flappy Bird game concept, selecting assets (images or sprites) that possess high creativity value, appealing to users of all age groups. Leveraging the Flutter framework, I skillfully developed this engaging game, building upon the provided template from the company. The game has been successfully published under the company's Google Play account, aiming to attract a wide audience of players.

Corporate Website : Penang Stall

I have successfully developed a website for an actual client who wanted to establish an online platform for their business, which involves creating custom stalls for entrepreneurs all over Malaysia. The website's content, including copywriting and images, was provided by the client. The focal point of the website is the prominent Click to Action (CTA) button, which seamlessly directs customers to Penang Stall's customer service via WhatsApp. I utilized WordPress and Elementor to craft this user-friendly and engaging website.

By offering augmented shopping experiences, customers can now visualize and interact with unique products in ways never seen before, enhancing their online shopping experience.
